Basic Steps to Play Top Trumps

  1. Deal the Cards – Cards are distributed evenly among players.
  2. Start the Round – Player 1 picks a category on their card and reads the value.
  3. Compare Values – Player 2 compares their cards value for the same category.
  4. Win Round – Player with highest value category collects the other card.
  5. Continue Play – The winner of the round picks the category in the next round.
  6. Win Game – Play continues until one player has all the cards.
